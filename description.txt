Adds achievements for world difficulties/special seeds to the in-game list.

This mod blocks the use of certain items based on the current world progression (e.g. you can't use post-Moon Lord items before defeating Moon Lord, etc.).

Rules:
- No Celebrationmk10 Seed
- No Journey Mode

Contribute language localization here: https://github.com/timenoe/Terraria-WorldAchievements
